About Us

School Software Pro is a product of MeredoxIT, a Software and ICT Support Company in Nigeria. We are supporting many Nigerian Schools in ICT Support and Computer business. We are supporting many educational institutions, Legal, engineering, educational and super store industries in Nigeria. In 2013, MeredoxIT launched renounce ERP software called Meredox School Management Software (MSMS) for schools, universities, polytechnics, seminaries, and other training institutions. School Software Pro as a product targeted to meet Nigerian Schools ICT needs, mainly Nursery, Primary and Secondary schools. School Software Pro help schools to teach, learn, examine and manage data. Meredox Ltd is looking forward to partnering with Local, State and Federal Governments of Nigeria in educational ICT. Our doors are open to help private firms, government and NGOs to increase efficiency and reduce their running cost through ICT.
